2024 Application

Applications due March 31st.
Winners announced April 25th.

Purpose

The purpose of the PIL Legacy Golf Scholarship is to help lower the barrier of entry to golf for student-athletes who show enthusiasm towards the game of golf.

Process

Each year, applications will be sent out to all the PIL coaches of both boys and girls golf programs. The student-athletes will have one month to complete the application. Applications will be reviewed by the coaches at the second to last tournament of the year. The applicant with the most votes will be presented with the scholarship at the District Tournament at the end of the year.

Scholarship Award

The winner of the PIL Legacy Golf Scholarship will be awarded a $1,000 gift certificate to RedTail Golf Center. The gift certificate will include a custom club fitting, and strongly encouraged to be used towards the purchase of a new set of irons.

Qualifications

The student-athlete must be a in their 1st, 2nd, or 3rd year of high school, meet all eligibility requirements to play team golf set by the PIL, and have shown continued interest in developing in the game of golf. Strong consideration will be given to student-athletes who need financial aid to buy their first set of custom-fitted irons.

2024 Application Form

Background Information & Golf Experience
In one paragraph, explain your experience with golf to date. Include how you got introduced to the game, and what you do currently for practice & play.

Extra Curriculars
List up to 3 extra-curricular activities you wish to include in your application.

Essay
This is your opportunity to explain what you think golf will mean to you for the remainder of your high school career and beyond. What does the game of golf look like in your future? What would it mean to receive the scholarship?
