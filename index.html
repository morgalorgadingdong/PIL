Scholarships

The winner of the PIL Legacy Golf Scholarship will be awarded a $1,000 gift certificate to RedTail Golf Center. The gift certificate will include a custom club fitting, and strongly encouraged to be used towards the purchase of a new set of irons.

Eligibility

The student-athlete must be a in their 1st, 2nd, or 3rd year of high school, meet all eligibility requirements to play team golf set by the PIL, and have shown continued interest in developing in the game of golf. Strong consideration will be given to student-athletes who need financial aid to buy their first set of custom-fitted irons.

Timeline

Applications will be accepted until March 31st, and the winner will be announced April 25th.

Process

Applications will be reviewed by the coaches at the second to last tournament of the year. The applicant with the most votes will be presented with the scholarship at the District Tournament at the end of the year.
